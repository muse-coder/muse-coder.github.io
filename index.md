## About Me

I am **Di Mou**. My work and research interests span **AI infrastructure**, **LLM systems**, and **GPU kernel optimization**. I am particularly interested in building efficient systems and tools for model inference and AI-assisted kernel engineering.

My previous research at the College of Computer Science, Chongqing University focused on coarse-grained reconfigurable architectures (CGRAs), stochastic computing, and hardware-software co-design.

## Research Interests

- **AI Infrastructure:** LLM systems, efficient inference, and agentic infrastructure
- **GPU Systems:** kernel generation, profiling, and performance optimization
- **Computer Architecture:** CGRA, stochastic computing, and hardware-software co-design
